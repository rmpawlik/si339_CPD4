SEC-HS Jamboree #2 (Red) Womens 5000 Meters Reserve Girls

Tue Sep 24 2024

Race Summary

The Skyline girls reserve cross country team delivered an impressive performance at the SEC-HS Jamboree #2, finishing strong in 2nd place overall. The team showcased their depth and determination, pushing through the competition to place right behind Saline.Gwen Stotts-Walshe led the charge, finishing with a personal record of 22:20.60.

Freshman Violet Olley clocked in at 23:07.20 for a personal best, followed closely by junior Adrienne Stewart at 23:07.70. Sophomore Pia Odell hit a personal best of 24:09.90, with junior Ruthie Scott finishing in 24:45.30, rounding out a solid team effort.
